Frequently Asked Questions

What are instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

  • If you have a HZ student pass you can park your car at the following address: Poelendaelesingel, 4335 JA Middelburg. You can enter the parking lot by scanning your HZ student pass at the entrance.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

  1. Log in on myhz.
  2. Click on webshop on your personal menu.
  3. Click on your desired item.
  4. Go to shopping cart.
  5. Press pay.
  6. Select a payment method.
  7. Pay.

How can you book a project space?

  1. Log in at my portal.hz.nl
  2. Edit the personal menu.
  3. Add the self service portal.
  4. Go to the self service portal.
  5. Click reserven

How can you print documents from your laptop at HZ?

  1. Go to the HZ website and login with your username and password.
  2. Click on Choose File and select file you want to print - repeating the step if you need to add more documents.
  3. After selecting all documents, tick the Advanced option to choose whether you want double-sided or black & white printing.
  4. Choose between the "HZ printer"/"HZ plotter".
  5. After requesting the print you must register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer and select "Print"
  6. In the menu, select the printer to which you requested the print.
  7. The print will be completed only if there is enough credit on your printing account.
  8. When everything is completed, press "Stop" on the TouchID, and lastly - "Logout".

How can you scan documents and dsend them to your laptop at HZ?

  1. Make sure you have a sufficient amount of money on your HZ card.
  2. Register using your HZ pass on the RFID reader next to the printer.
  3. In the printer menu, select option Scanner.
  4. Place the original sheet on the feeder or glass plate.
  5. Press send to me.
  6. Press Yes.
  7. Preess the Start Button.
  8. Press the green Start button on the printer to scan page by page.
  9. Once this is ready, press Start Sending.
  10. Log out of the printer
